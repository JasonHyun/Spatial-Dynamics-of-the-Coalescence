## Coalescent Compliance Validation

All reconstructed trees follow standard coalescent principles:

- **Tree Structure**: Internal nodes represent coalescent events, leaf nodes represent lineages
- **Coalescent Process**: Exponential waiting times with k(k-1)/(2Ne) rates
- **Random Coalescence**: Lineages randomly selected for coalescence
- **VAF Integration**: VAF used for Ne estimation and lineage mapping
- **Biological Context**: Mutations mapped to lineages preserving evolutionary information

## Interpretation

**Successful reconstructions** provide coalescent-compliant trees that:
- Follow standard coalescent theory principles
- Use VAF information for biological interpretation
- Enable both mathematical and biological analysis
- Preserve evolutionary context of mutations

**Error cases** typically result from:
- Insufficient mutations (< 3) for reliable analysis
- File reading or parsing issues
- Data quality problems
